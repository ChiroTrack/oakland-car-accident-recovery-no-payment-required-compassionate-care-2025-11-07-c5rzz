About The Shift Chiropractic

The Shift Chiropractic was founded on the principle that accident victims deserve immediate, compassionate, and expert care without financial burden. Our practice began in 2015 when our founder, Dr. Michael Torres, witnessed too many patients suffering from untreated auto accident injuries because they couldn't afford upfront medical costs. He envisioned a clinic that would remove financial barriers to healing while providing world-class chiropractic and rehabilitative services. Today, we've grown to serve thousands of Oakland residents and remain committed to our founding mission: making quality accident injury care accessible to everyone who needs it.

Our core values—compassion, expertise, accessibility, and integrity—guide every decision we make. We believe that healing is both a physical and emotional journey, and we're honored to support our patients through every step. Our team of board-certified doctors, multilingual staff, and dedicated support professionals work collaboratively to provide personalized care that addresses not just the injury, but the whole person. We're deeply rooted in the Oakland community, and we take pride in being the trusted choice for auto accident recovery among residents from all walks of life.
